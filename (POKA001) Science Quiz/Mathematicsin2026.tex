    \chapter{Mathematics in 2026}

    This part is a working list of mathematical events, awards, and recent
    developments from the current Science Quiz season. It is meant to be
    replaced each year.

    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{2025 Shaw Prize in Mathematical Sciences.} Kenji Fukaya was
              awarded the 2025 Shaw Prize in Mathematical Sciences for his work
              in symplectic geometry, the Fukaya category, symplectic topology,
              mirror symmetry, and gauge theory.

        \item \textbf{2025 AI and the IMO.} In July 2025, AI systems from Google
              DeepMind and OpenAI reached gold-medal-level scores on the
              International Mathematical Olympiad problems. Quiz keywords:
              mathematical reasoning, olympiad problems, natural-language
              solutions, AI-assisted mathematics.

        \item \textbf{2026 ICM.} The 2026 International Congress of
              Mathematicians is scheduled for Philadelphia, USA, from July 23 to
              July 30, 2026. The 20th IMU General Assembly is scheduled for New
              York City from July 20 to July 21, 2026.

        \item \textbf{2026 IMU prizes at ICM.} The ICM 2026 opening ceremony is
              the key moment to check the Fields Medals, the IMU Abacus Medal,
              the Gauss Prize, and the Chern Medal Award. The Leelavati Prize
              is connected to the closing ceremony.

        \item \textbf{2026 Fields Medals.} The 2026 Fields Medalists should be
              checked immediately after the ICM 2026 opening ceremony.

        \item \textbf{2026 Abel Prize.} Gerd Faltings received the 2026 Abel
              Prize. Quiz keywords: arithmetic geometry, Mordell conjecture,
              Faltings' theorem, Mordell--Lang conjecture, Diophantine
              geometry.

        \item \textbf{2026 Breakthrough Prize in Mathematics.} Frank Merle
              received the 2026 Breakthrough Prize in Mathematics. Quiz
              keywords: nonlinear evolution equations, stability, singularity
              formation, solitons.

        \item \textbf{2026 Shaw Prize in Mathematical Sciences.} Emmanuel
              Cand\`es and Camillo De Lellis received the 2026 Shaw Prize in
              Mathematical Sciences. Quiz keywords: applied harmonic analysis,
              compressed sensing, signal processing, statistics, geometric
              measure theory, singularities, fluid dynamics.

        \item \textbf{2026 Clay Research Awards.} The 2026 Clay Research Awards
              were made to three groups: Tuomas Orponen, Pablo Shmerkin, Hong
              Wang, and Joshua Zahl; Robert Burklund, Jeremy Hahn, Ishan Levy,
              and Tomer Schlank; and Yu Deng and Zaher Hani. Quiz keywords:
              Furstenberg sets, Kakeya conjecture, Telescope Conjecture,
              Boltzmann equation.

        \item \textbf{2026 Wolf Prize in Mathematics.} The 2026 Wolf Prize is in
              a mathematics-awarding year. Check the official Wolf Foundation
              announcement before the first-review edition and again before
              printing.

        \item \textbf{Korea-related current issues.} Add Korea-related
              mathematics news, including major prizes, appointments, deaths,
              and famous young mathematicians, after checking official sources.

        \item \textbf{\textcolor{postechred}{POSTECH}--\textcolor{kaistblue}{KAIST} Science War.}
              Add 2026 season-specific trends after new practice problems,
              team notes, and actual match information become available.
    \end{itemize}
